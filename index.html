About Kudi App

Kudi app is a digital platform developed and managed by Kudi Business, with the purpose of facilitating citizens' access to gastronomic, hotel, cultural and leisure experiences in Angola.

With an intuitive, modern and georeferenced interface, Kudi connects users to the best options with just one click, providing fluid navigation, personalized recommendations, and quick access to the most relevant information based on the user's location.

Privacy Policy

1 Data Collection

The Kudi app may collect the following data from users, with explicit consent:

  • Registration information: name, e-mail, phone number, location (via GPS), and usage preferences.
  • Browsing data: interactions with the app, search history, places visited, devices, and operating system.
  • Third-party information: when authorized by the user (e.g., login via social networks).

2 Use of Information

The data collected is used to:

  • Personalize the user experience on the platform.
  • Nominate the most relevant restaurants, hotels, events, and attractions based on location and preferences.
  • Send relevant notifications (promotions, news, and recommendations).
  • Improve services, through statistical analysis and machine learning.
  • Support strategic decisions of business partners, always anonymously.

3 Storage and Protection

  • The data is stored on secure servers, with restricted access and encryption.
  • We apply strict information security practices to prevent unauthorized access, leaks, or data loss.
  • The data is kept only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

4 Data Sharing

Kudi Business may share data with:

  • Business partners, as long as they are anonymized or with explicit consent from the user.
  • Legal authorities, when required by law or by court order.
  • Contracted service providers, responsible for operating parts of the technological infrastructure.

5 Consent and Rights of Users

The user has the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing.
  • Request data portability.

Consent will be requested at the time of installation and use of the app. Failure to provide certain information may limit the user experience.

6 Updates

This Privacy Policy may be amended from time to time. The date of the last update will always be indicated at the beginning of the document. We recommend reading regularly to be aware of possible changes.
