Account Deletion Request

Here you can request the deletion of your Kudi account and associated data. Please read the instructions carefully before proceeding with your request.

How to Request Account Deletion

1 Contact Support

Currently, account deletion requests can only be processed by contacting our support team directly. Please send an email with the required information to initiate the account deletion process.

We're working on implementing an in-app account deletion feature for a more streamlined experience in the future.

2 Send Request by Email

Send an email to our support team with the subject: "Account Deletion Request - Kudi" and include:

  • Full name used on the account
  • Email address associated with the account
  • Reason for the request (optional)
Contact Support Team

The email will be sent to gouveiamartins6@gmail.com. Please make sure to include all required information for faster processing.

3 What Gets Deleted

  • Authentication credentials: we will remove the user from Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile and preferences data: all Firestore/Realtime Database documents linked to the user UID will be deleted (e.g., preferences, visit history, favorites).
  • User-generated content: comments, reviews, and uploads will be deleted if directly tied to the UID (or anonymized, if required).

4 Data Retained and Retention Period

For legal and security reasons, we may retain certain technical logs for a limited period:

  • System logs (technical records): retained up to 30 days for auditing and security.
  • Aggregated and anonymized data for statistics: retained indefinitely without identifying users.

5 Processing Time

After confirming your request, we will delete your data within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

6 Notes on Firebase Authentication (OAuth)

Kudi uses Firebase Authentication (including OAuth via Google, Facebook, or email). When an account is deleted:

  • The authentication account is removed from Firebase Authentication.
  • Associated data stored in Firestore/Realtime DB is deleted as outlined above.
  • If you used third-party login (e.g., Google), you can also revoke the permission directly in your external provider account.
Important:

Before submitting your request, make sure you have access to the email linked to your account (needed to confirm your identity). If you don't have access, contact support and provide as much information as possible so we can verify account ownership.
